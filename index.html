Short Bio

Shuhao Zhang is a Ph.D. student (defense passed) in school of computing at National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He. He obtained his B.E(1st hons) degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2014. From Aug. 2014 to July. 2018, he worked as a research trainee (Ph.D. Scholar) in SAP Innovation Center Network, Singapore, under the supervision of Dr. Daniel Dahlmeier. From Aug. 2018 to Aug. 2019, he worked as a research assistant in National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He. From Sep. 2019, he works as research scientist II in I2R Astar. He had published a list of papers in top-tier conferences and journals including SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, SC and TPDS. He had served as PC member of CIKM2019, and invited reviewers of TCC and ICPP.

Research Interests

My primary research areas are Database and Big Data Management and Analytics. Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Data Stream Processing and Mining on Modern Hardware
  • High Performance Confidential Stream Data Processing
  • Timeseries and Trajectory Data Management and Processing on Modern Hardware

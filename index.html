Short Bio

Shuhao Zhang is currently postdoctoral researcher in Technische Universität Berlin working with Prof.Volker Markl. He obtained his Ph.D. degree from the school of computing at National University of Singapore in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He. He obtained his B.E (1st hons) degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2014. From Aug. 2014 to July. 2018, he worked as a research trainee (Ph.D. Scholar) in SAP Innovation Center Network, Singapore, under the supervision of Dr. Daniel Dahlmeier. From Aug. 2018 to Aug. 2019, he worked as a research assistant in National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He.
He had published a list of papers in top-tier conferences and journals including SIGMOD, VLDB, ICDE, SC and TPDS. He had served as PC member of CIKM 2019, CCIS 2019 and ICDCS 2020.

Research Interests

My primary research areas are Database Systems and Big Data Management and Analytics (ML?1). Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Data Stream Processing and Mining on Modern Hardware
  • High Performance Data Processing on Trusted Computing Platforms
  • Timeseries and Trajectory Data Management

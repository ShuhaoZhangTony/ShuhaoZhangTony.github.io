Short Bio

Mr. Shuhao Zhang is a fifth year Ph.D. candiate in school of computing at National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He. He obtained his B.E(1st hons) degree from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2014. From Aug. 2014 to Aug. 2018, he worked as a research trainee (Ph.D. Scholar) in SAP Innovation Center Network, Singapore, under the supervision of Dr. Daniel Dahlmeier. Starting from Aug. 2018, he work as a research fellow/assistant in National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He.

Research Interests

My primary research areas are Database and Big Data Management and Analytics. Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Data Stream Processing and Mining
  • Scalable Complex Event Processing
  • Hardware-conscious Query Processing
  • Timeseries Management
  • Spatial and Spatio-Temporal Databases

Shuhao Zhang is currently a postdoctoral researcher at Technische Universität Berlin working with Prof.Volker Markl on the topic of data stream processing and management.

He obtained his Ph.D. degree from the school of computing at National University of Singapore in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Bingsheng He.

His current research interest include big data management systems (with special interests in stream processing and timeseries management), distributed and parallel computing and emerging ML/AI applications.
